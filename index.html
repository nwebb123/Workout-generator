PPL Workout Generator

The purpose of this web app is to generate a daily workout for someone following what's known as a Push/Pull/Legs (PPL) split. According to Men's Health, PPL is a full-body training split that's designed to build muscle in almost every single muscle group by following a simple format: on a certain day of the week, you only train either upper-body pushing muscles (push), upper-body pulling muscles (pull), or your entire lower-body (legs). A common weekly schedule might look something like this:

Monday Tuesday Wednesday Thursday Friday Saturday Sunday
Week 1: Push Pull Legs Rest Push Pull Legs
Week 2: Rest Push Rest Pull Legs Rest Push

Muscle groups being targeted: Chest, front delts, and triceps.

Muscle groups being targeted: Back, traps, and biceps.

Muscle groups being targeted: Legs, calves.

Abdominal muscles are resilient, and respond well to more frequent training.

Cardio can vary from simple walks to rigorous hiking.
